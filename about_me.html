Hi! I'm Thomas.

My Name is Thomas, and I was born in Halifax, Nova Scotia. I spent most of my life there, up until 2014; I’ve been back and forth between Korea since then. I originally went to (Dalhousie) university thinking I was going to be a doctor, but ended up studying psychology. My fascination with people and social interactions drew me to psychology, and its still what drives my career and hobbies.

Teaching

I’m currently employed as an English teacher in South Korea. I work teaching kindergarten, elementary, and adult students. I enjoy the variety of levels as it keeps me on my toes, and I get to try out a lot of different teaching techniques. That being said, teaching adults is my favorite! I love teaching grammar and conversational English to adults; They are self-motivated and we are able to have a lot of great, interesting discussions. I come to think of students as more like friends than students; It makes the atmosphere a lot more relaxed, and the learning becomes a lot more flexible and natural. Photography

My passion for photography, and portrait photography in particular, grew after meeting a Korean photographer. I did a little modeling for them; I used to be so nervous in front of the camera and thought I always looked bad. BUT! They were able to get some nice photos of me, which really boosted my confidence. It was weird to see a model like picture of me. So, I want to be able to do that for other people too. I believe everyone should have photos of themselves that make them feel empowered, and good about themselves. I hope to be able to take photos like that for a great many people!
